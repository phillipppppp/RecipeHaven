Piaparan

Piaparan is a Maranao dish made with chicken stewed in coconut milk, turmeric, and shredded coconut for a fragrant and rich flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Garlic, onion, and ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  2. Stir in turmeric and shredded coconut. Pour in coconut milk.
  3. Simmer until chicken is fully cooked and sauce thickens.
