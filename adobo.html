Chicken Adobo

A savory and slightly tangy Filipino favorite from Luzon.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into serving pieces)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and sauté the marinated chicken until lightly browned.
  3. Add vinegar, bay leaves, peppercorns, and water. Do not stir.
  4. Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer for 30–40 minutes.
  5. Add sugar if desired. Simmer until sauce thickens.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice.
