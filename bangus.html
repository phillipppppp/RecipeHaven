Rellenong Bangus

Stuffed Milkfish from the Philippines

Ingredients

  • 1 large bangus (milkfish), cleaned and deboned
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup carrots, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil fish until soft. Remove meat and flake. Set skin aside.
  2. Saut√© garlic and onion, add fish meat, carrots, peas, salt, pepper.
  3. Let it cool, mix in beaten egg.
  4. Stuff the mixture back into the fish skin.
  5. Wrap in foil and bake or fry until golden.
