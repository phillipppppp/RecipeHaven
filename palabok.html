Palabok

A festive noodle dish smothered in rich orange sauce

Ingredients

  • 500g rice noodles (bihon)
  • 2 cups shrimp broth
  • 2 tbsp annatto powder
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch (dissolved in water)
  • 1/2 cup ground pork
  • 1/2 cup tinapa flakes (smoked fish)
  • Hard-boiled eggs, shrimp, chicharon, green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook bihon noodles per package instructions and set aside.
  2. Saut√© ground pork, then add shrimp broth and annatto powder.
  3. Stir in cornstarch slurry and cook until sauce thickens.
  4. Top noodles with sauce, tinapa, eggs, shrimp, chicharon, and green onions.
