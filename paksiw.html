Paksiw na Pata

Paksiw na Pata is a Filipino pork hock stew simmered in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and dried banana blossoms. Itâ€™s tangy, savory, and richly comforting.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork hock (pata)
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/4 cup dried banana blossoms
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. In a pot, combine pork hock, garlic, onion, vinegar, soy sauce, peppercorns, and bay leaves. Let boil.
  2. Add water to cover meat and simmer until tender.
  3. Add banana blossoms and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  4. Adjust seasoning and serve hot.
