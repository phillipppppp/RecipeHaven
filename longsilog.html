Longsilog

A sweet and savory Filipino breakfast classic

Ingredients

  • 3 pieces Filipino longganisa (sweet sausage)
  • 2 cups cooked garlic rice
  • 2 eggs, sunny-side up
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Vinegar or tomato ketchup (optional, for dipping)

Instructions

  1. Cook longganisa in a pan over low heat with a splash of water. Let it simmer until water evaporates, then fry until browned.
  2. In a separate pan, fry the eggs to your preference.
  3. Reheat or prepare garlic rice and plate it with longganisa and egg.
  4. Serve hot with dipping sauce if desired.
