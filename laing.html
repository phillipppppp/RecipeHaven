Laing

Taro leaves in rich coconut milk

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried taro leaves
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 cup dried fish or pork
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Chili peppers (optional)

Instructions

  1. Simmer coconut milk with garlic and meat.
  2. Add taro leaves without stirring and cook until soft.
  3. Pour coconut cream and simmer until thick.
  4. Add chili if desired. Serve hot.
