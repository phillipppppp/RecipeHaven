Filipino Egg Pie

A rich and creamy custard-filled pie with a toasty top.

Ingredients:

  • 1 9-inch pie crust (prebaked)
  • 1 can (300ml) condensed milk
  • 1 can (370ml) evaporated milk
  • 4 eggs + 1 egg white
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk 4 eggs, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla.
  3. Strain mixture into the prebaked pie crust.
  4. Beat the extra egg white until frothy and gently pour over the top.
  5. Bake for 45–50 minutes or until set and top is browned.
  6. Cool completely before slicing. Serve chilled or room temp.
