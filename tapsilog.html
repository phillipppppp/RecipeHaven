Tapsilog

A Filipino breakfast classic with tapa, sinangag, and itlog!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg beef tapa, marinated
  • 3 cups garlic fried rice
  • 3 eggs, fried sunny side up
  • Vinegar dipping sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the marinated beef tapa until browned and tender.
  2. Prepare garlic fried rice in a separate pan.
  3. Fry eggs sunny side up.
  4. Serve together on a plate. Add vinegar on the side if desired.
