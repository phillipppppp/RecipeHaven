Lugaw

A soothing Filipino rice porridge perfect for any time of day

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • 6 cups water or chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-size ginger, sliced
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Hard-boiled egg, toasted garlic, and spring onions for topping

Instructions

  1. Saut√© garlic and ginger in a pot until fragrant.
  2. Add rice and water or broth. Bring to a boil then simmer until rice is soft and porridge-like.
  3. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper.
  4. Top with egg, garlic, and spring onions before serving.
