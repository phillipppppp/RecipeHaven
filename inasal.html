Chicken Inasal

Chicken Inasal is a popular grilled chicken dish from Bacolod marinated in calamansi, lemongrass, garlic, vinegar, and annatto oil.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup annatto oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  2. Grill chicken over medium heat, basting with annatto oil until cooked.
  3. Serve with garlic rice and dipping sauce.
