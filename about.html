About Recipe Haven

Let us help you find what your taste buds are looking for


Hi! We are Lance and Phillip two food lovers who share the same passion for cooking, and mostly eating..This food blog aims to teach young aspiring cooks different kind of recipes to satisfy their taste buds.


We mainly want to share different kinds of home cooked filipino meals, may it be breakfast, lunch, meryenda, or dinner. We believe that cooking should be fun and flavorful, and that’s why we’re here to help!


We’ll be sharing our favorite family recipes, sprinkled with a bit of our own twist. Each category will include easy-to-follow steps, plus some tips and tricks we’ve learned along the way. We hope to inspire you to get into the kitchen and whip up something delicious for your loved ones. So join us on this tasty journey, and let’s make cooking an adventure together
